Shibo Zhang

I am a computer science PhD student at Northwestern University, HABitsLab, where I work on pervasive and ubiquitous computing. I received my bachelor degree at Harbin Institute of Technology.

Email  /  Biography  /  Google Scholar  /  Twitter

Research

My research area is human activity recognition and mobile health. I'm interested in applying machine learning and deep learning techniques to help advance our ability to perceive and understand human behaviors, and ultimately prevent problematic health habits. I also have interest and some experience in computer vision and speech recognition.
